Supporters & Donors

Community Library

  • Leadership in Education Award 2019 by Delhi Institute of Management in Association with Niti Aayog.
  • Healthcare Leadership Award 2019 by Zee Business.
  • 51 Most Impactful Social Innovators of 2019 Award by World CSR Federation.
  • Beti Bachao, Beti Padhao Award 2018 for active participation in good governance by Ramraj.
  • Best NGO Award 2017-18 by India Habitat Centre.
  • Nayi Soch Award 2013 by Star Plus for innovative initiative of providing after school academic support.

Recognition

  • Mrs. Rani Patel, Founder and President of Aarohan was recognized as one amongst 5 Women Achievers by Jamia Milia Islamia and honoured by Ms Najma Akhtar (Vice Chancellor) on 27th August 2019.
  • University of Alabama sent a team of Professors and 40 students to study Aarohan’s holistic model of ‘childhood to livelihood’ for two consecutive years in July 2018 and July 2019.
  • Recognition by ARNEC in their Conference Journal at Nepal in March 2018 – organized by UNICEF, UNESCO and ARNEC. Aarohan’s initiatives in early childhood care, education and holistic support from childhood to livelihood was included in the Journal.
  • Presentation on Advocacy and Early Childhood Care and Development in Nov 2018 at Ambedkar University.
  • Save the Children Organization visited with a team of 5 NGOs from Nepal in 2017 to study the Early Childhood Care Centre of Aarohan.
  • Humanity Magazine, Singapore wrote an article on Aarohan in 2014.
  • MCMILAN Advanced Research Series (acknowledged by Jamia Milia Islamia University) published, “Public Policy Reforms on Development: Challenges before Emerging Economies” in October 2013
